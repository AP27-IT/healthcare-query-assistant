Hospital Emergency Care Policy

1. Emergency Treatment Guarantee
The hospital provides emergency medical treatment to any patient presenting
with an emergency condition regardless of insurance status or ability to pay,
in accordance with emergency care regulations. Stabilizing treatment is never
delayed pending insurance verification.

2. Triage Process
All emergency patients are triaged by a qualified nurse immediately upon
arrival. Triage priority is assigned based on the severity of the condition,
not order of arrival. Life-threatening conditions receive immediate
attention.

3. Emergency Admission
Patients requiring further treatment following emergency stabilization are
admitted under the Emergency admission type. Emergency admissions bypass the
standard scheduling process and are prioritized for bed assignment.

4. Notification of Family or Guardian
For patients unable to communicate, the emergency team will make reasonable
efforts to contact next of kin or a legal guardian as soon as possible after
stabilization.

5. Post-Emergency Follow-Up
Patients discharged from the emergency department receive written discharge
instructions and, where appropriate, a referral for follow-up care with a
specialist or primary care physician.
