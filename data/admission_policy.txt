Hospital Admission Policy

1. General Admission Process
Patients may be admitted under three categories: Elective, Urgent, or Emergency.
Elective admissions require a scheduled appointment and pre-admission clearance
from the attending physician at least 48 hours in advance. Urgent admissions
require physician referral and are processed within 24 hours. Emergency
admissions are accepted immediately without prior scheduling, through the
Emergency Department, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

2. Required Documentation
All patients must present a valid government-issued photo ID and their
insurance card at the time of admission. For elective admissions, patients
must also provide a referral letter and any relevant prior medical records.

3. Room Assignment
Room assignment is based on medical necessity, bed availability, and patient
preference where possible. Isolation rooms are reserved for patients with
infectious conditions as determined by the attending physician.

4. Minors and Guardianship
Patients under 18 years of age must be accompanied by a parent or legal
guardian who is authorized to consent to treatment on the patient's behalf.

5. Admission Fees
A standard admission processing fee applies to all Elective admissions and is
billed separately from treatment costs. Emergency and Urgent admissions are
exempt from this processing fee.
